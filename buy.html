The vendors at our show come from all across the Midwest, each bringing their unique interests, backgrounds, and expertise.


As a result, what they bring to the table each show is ever-changing. That said, here’s our attempt to summarize a show full of delightful surprises.

The show will have a mixture of both modern and vintage gear. This includes everything from mirrorless and DSLR gear to antique analog cameras and lenses.

Explore a diverse selection of brands, styles, and gear types, where you'll discover timeless classics alongside hard-to-find lenses and accessories. Each show is a thrilling treasure hunt, with something new and unexpected at every turn.

While cameras and lenses are a major focus of our event, they are just the beginning. You'll also find a wide variety of other photographic gear, including darkroom equipment, tripods, bags, straps, caps, photos, lighting, and much more.
