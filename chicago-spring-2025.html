About The Show

Buy cameras, lenses, & more

When it comes to buying equipment, this show offers something for everyone—from  beginners to seasoned photographers. With a diverse range of vendors, you’ll find both modern and vintage gear, ensuring you can discover the perfect pieces for your collection or photography setup. Whether you’re searching for a top-of-the-line mirrorless camera, a classic film lens, or even a rare accessory, the show is the ideal place to find unique and quality gear at competitive prices. Plus, with experts on hand to offer advice and answer questions, you can shop with confidence knowing you’re making informed choices.

Beyond cameras and lenses, you will also find other photographic equipment from darkroom equipment and film to lighting, tripods, caps, grips, and more.

Are you hoping to see a specific camera, lens, or other accessory? Let us know.

Sell your unused camera equipment

Got unused camera equipment? Bring it to the show and sell it to our trusted vendors for instant cash. It's a fast and easy way to turn your old gear into money.

If you are looking to upgrade what you have, trade your old equipment for something new. Whether it’s a camera, lens, or accessory, our vendors are ready to help you refresh your collection and get exactly what you need.

Have a lot of gear to sell? Whether it is a collection you are looking to part with or an inheritance of gear that you are not sure what to do with, a table may be the right choice for you. To inquire about a table, please fill out this form.

Connect with fellow photographers & camera enthusiasts

The show is the perfect place to meet and network with photographers and collectors from Chicago and across the Midwest. Whether you're looking to share tips, trade gear, or just chat about your latest projects, you'll find a community of like-minded individuals who share your passion for cameras. Don’t miss the chance to make new connections, exchange ideas, and grow your network in a welcoming and creative environment.

Take a look at some of the people who make the shows.

A treasure trove for collectors

For collectors, the show can be a goldmine of rare and unique finds. Explore a curated selection of esoteric cameras, vintage lenses, and hard-to-find accessories—items you won’t discover in a typical camera store. From obscure models to historical gems, it's like stepping into a living museum of photographic history. Whether you're hunting for a specific piece to complete your collection or just enjoy browsing the unusual, you'll find treasures that are truly one-of-a-kind.

FAQ

Is there an entry fee?

Yes. There is a $5 entry fee for each person and a discounted $3 entry fee for students.
Tickets are only available for purchase at the door.

Is the event cash only?

All vendors will accept cash, however some do take credit card or other virtual forms of payment. There is an ATM at the venue for those who only take cash.

Is this space wheelchair accessible?

Yes. Parking is free at the hotel and includes wheelchair accessible spaces. The event is located on the first floor and is accessible by wheelchair from the parking lot. Within the event, tables allot enough space for wheelchair users and those with other mobility devices to explore the even with ease.

Are new photographers welcome to this event?

Absolutely! This is a great opportunity to find a reliable first setup at an affordable price. While we offer a wide range of equipment, many of our dealers also bring some entry-level gear, as helping someone get started with their first camera, flash, darkroom set up, etc. is one of the most rewarding aspects of the show. Please don't feel intimidated, we will meet you where you are at on your photography journey and help you find what you are looking for.

Can large groups attend?

Large groups are always welcome to attend. In the past, we have had college classes, photography groups, and other organizations take a shuttle to the event. If you are interested in coordinating this for your group, please feel free to contact us.
